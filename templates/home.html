About This Project

AI Doctor is an educational AI assistant built to explore the intersection of speech recognition, computer vision, and large language models within a clinical context. It is designed to simulate doctor-style consultations using real-time multimodal inputs.

The application leverages Groq's inference infrastructure for both audio transcription and multimodal reasoning. Responses are delivered as structured text and converted to speech for an immersive, accessible experience. User accounts and interaction history are stored securely in MongoDB.

This project demonstrates how modern AI stacks — combining STT, vision-language models, and TTS — can be orchestrated into a coherent, real-time medical workflow. It is intended strictly for research and educational purposes.

Disclaimer: AI Doctor is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.
